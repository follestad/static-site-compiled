Recording studio in Oslo, Norway.

Music, sound and audio recording in the heart of Oslo.

In downtown Oslo, just around the corner of Youngstorget, you’ll find Cantus Studio. A music studio fully equipped with loads of analog, digital and vintage recording equipment and instruments. If you’re in need of audio or music recording and mixing, you’ve come to the right place.
