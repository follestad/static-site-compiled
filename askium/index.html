Askium draws inspiration from the ash tree in Norse mythology, a symbol of growth, strength, and connection, and Asclepius, the Greek god of health and healing, embodying balance and well-being. With the “-ium” suffix evoking the elements of the periodic table, Askium stands for something essential, enduring, and rooted in both nature and knowledge.

As a consulting firm, Askium partners with companies in health, medicine, production, and research, bringing expertise and innovation to drive progress and resilience. We blend tradition, science, and collaboration to create sustainable solutions for the future.
